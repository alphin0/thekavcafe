Our Story

A Sanctuary in the City

Welcome to KAV, a green bistro shaped by a passion for freshness, wellness, and mindful dining. We believe food should do more than satisfy hunger — it should create a sense of balance, comfort, and connection.

The KAV is a green bistro and café in North Paravur, Kerala, located near Thonniyakavu Temple. We serve thoughtfully prepared coffee and bistro-style dishes in a quiet, nature-inspired setting designed for relaxed, unhurried dining.
